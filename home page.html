About Us

Dettol is a British brand of cleaning supplies and disinfectant owned by Reckitt Benckiser. Invented in 1933, they sell a variety of different forms of household sanitary cleaners, ranging from thin mist spray disinfectant to wipes. They come with different scents.The original Dettol Antiseptic Disinfectant Liquid contains the antiseptic chloroxylenol. Dettol Antibacterial Surface Cleanser and Dettol Antibacterial Wipes contain the active ingredient. It is used for Use Dettol Antiseptic Liquid to kill germs on the skin, help protect against infection from cuts, scratches, and insect bites ... and it can also be used as a household disinfectant on surfaces or in the laundry. it is made of The active ingredient in Dettol is chloroxylenol B.P. 4.8%w/v. The other ingredients include isopropyl alcohol, pine oil, castor oil soap, caramel,and water.These ingredients make Dettol an effective antiseptic-disinfectant that kills bacteria, according to the Chemistryinyourcupboard website.
